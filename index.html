AI & Privacy Policy

What Qualia AI does:

Qualia looks at the colors your camera sees and puts them to work for you: adjusting them for your color vision, suggesting outfit combos, checking if fruit is ripe, and reading skin tones.

How your data is processed:

Your privacy is our highest priority. All image sampling and color analysis run 100% on-device locally. We do not transmit your camera feeds or images to any external servers or clouds. No telemetry or photos are saved without your consent.
